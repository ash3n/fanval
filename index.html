Welcome to Fantasy Valorant

In Fantasy Valorant, you can build your dream team of professional Valorant players across different regions. Points are scored based on the real-life performances of your selected players in professional matches. Play in normal draft, cross-regional draft, salary cap draft, or a custom league of your choosing with countless possibilities. Manage your roster throughout the season, trade players, and compete against friends for the glory of the top spot in the league.
